Medication Management Training

Medication Management Training is a Board of Nursing approved training for persons who assist with medications in facilities licensed by the Department of Behavioral Health and Developmental Services, including Mental Health, Mental Retardation and Substance Abuse Services.

Training will be provided by a contracted licensed nurse. Please note that this class does not include the additional 45 hours required for G-tube services. This is a 32-hour course and attendance at all classes is required for certification. Cost: $150, non-refundable.

Person Centered Training

Person Centered Thinking is training that will aid in developing an individualized support plan that is both relevant and meaningful to that individual. The curriculum for this course was developed by Michael Smull and the Essential Lifestyle Planning (ELP) Community. ELP has identified basic skills and tools that help learners understand and embrace this way of thinking. Teaching and supporting the use of Person Centered Thinking skills ensure that plans are carried out to the fullest and most effective potential.
