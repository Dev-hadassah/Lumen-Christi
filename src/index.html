• Service

Comprehensive IDD
Residential Support

Lumen Christi Home provides professional and compassionate care for individuals with Intellectual and Developmental Disabilities. Our services focus on creating an environment where residents feel safe, valued, and empowered to lead fulfilling lives.

Our Core Services Include

  • 24/7 Professional Support – Trained caregivers providing round-the-clock assistance.

  • Daily Living Assistance – Help with personal care, medication management, and daily routines.

  • Community Engagement – Residents participate in social outings, volunteer programs, and recreational activities to foster community connections.

  • Skill Development & Independence – Programs designed to encourage self-sufficiency and confidence in daily tasks.

  • Safe & Comfortable Housing – A home-like setting with structured routines to ensure stability and well-being.
